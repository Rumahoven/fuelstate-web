Delete Your Account

Enter the email address associated with your Fuelstate account. We'll send you a secure link to confirm and complete the deletion.

What will be deleted
  • Athlete profile and biometric data
  • All run records and fuel event history
  • Saved runs and carb loading plans
  • Product preferences and custom products
  • Account credentials
What we cannot delete on your behalf
  • Apple Health workouts — remove manually in the Health app
  • RevenueCat purchase history — retained for Apple billing compliance
  • Sentry crash reports — auto-purged within ~90 days
📬

Check your email

If an account exists for that address, you'll receive a confirmation link within a few minutes.

Click the link to permanently delete your account.

Faster option: If you have the app, go to Settings → Delete Account to delete everything instantly.
